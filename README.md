# 📊 RappiPlus Business Analysis

## 📖 Description

This project presents a comprehensive business analysis of **RappiPlus**, using Python for data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), data visualization, and statistical analysis. The objective is to transform raw business data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Rappi needed to better understand the performance of its RappiPlus service by analyzing customer behavior, sales, marketing investment, and operational metrics. The available information required cleaning, validation, and analysis before it could be used for business decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Solution

To address this problem, I:

- Imported and explored multiple datasets.
- Performed data cleaning and preprocessing.
- Handled missing values and duplicate records.
- Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Created visualizations to identify trends and patterns.
- Calculated key business metrics.
- Applied statistical analysis to support business conclusions.
- Summarized findings and proposed business recommendations.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Optimize marketing investment by focusing on the highest-performing channels.
- Continue monitoring key business metrics through dashboards.
- Improve data quality processes to ensure reliable reporting.
- Use customer behavior insights to strengthen retention strategies.
